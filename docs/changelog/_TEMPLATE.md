# Changelog Entry Template — `docs/changelog/`

> **Instructions for the Agent:** Fill in each section below. Keep the file short — aim for 20 to 40 lines total. No fluff, no padding. This content will reflect the contents of a given commit message.

> **File Naming with this structure --> `YYYY-MM-DD_feature-name.md`** (feature/fix/refactor depending on what was done)

Follow this structure:

***

## `YYYY-MM-DD` — [One-line title: what changed]

**Type:** `feature` | `fix` | `refactor` | `chore` _(pick one)_
**Branch:** `main`
**Status:** `✅ Merged` | `🔄 In Progress`

***

### Problem / Goal

_What was broken, missing, or needed? State the why in 2–3 sentences max. Write it so a non-developer could understand the pain point._

### Solution

_What was built or fixed? Describe the approach in 2–4 sentences. Mention the key technical decision if relevant, but keep it accessible._

### What Changed

- Short bullet of a concrete change
- Short bullet of a concrete change
- Short bullet of a concrete change _(3–5 bullets max)_

### Impact

_One sentence on what this enables for the user or the product. Think: "Now users can..." or "This unlocks..."_
